Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup Oats
  • Salt
  • 1/2 scoop protein powder
  • 3/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 Tbs Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tbs Maple Syrup

Directions

  1. Combine oats, yogurt, protein powder to tuppy
  2. Add milk until combined
  3. Place in fridge for 8 hours min
  4. Top with banana, peanut butter, and salt and maple
